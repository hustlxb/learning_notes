1. under the table  秘密，私下（进行违法的金钱交易）

例句：
Payments were made under the table to local officials.
当地官员接受了金钱贿赂。

2. turn the tables (on somebody)  （情况、局面）翻转

例句：
The tables were turned in the second half, when Leeds United scored from the penalty spot.
当利兹联队从罚球点得分时，下半场的赛况翻转了。

3. at table vs at the table

at table 泛指在吃饭这样一个状态哦
例句：
I thought you are at table now.
我还以为你们现在正吃着饭呢。

at the table 则特指在桌子边上哦
例句：
A: Where is little Jorge? 
A：小乔治在哪呢？
B: See? He is just sitting at the table. 
B：看那不是吗？桌子边坐着呢。
